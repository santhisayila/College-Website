About Us

Prasad V Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology, established in 1998, is a pioneering self-financed institution renowned for academic excellence and discipline. The college is sponsored by Siddhartha Academy of General and Technical Education, running 18 educational institutions across diverse fields of study.

  • Offers B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, and Ph.D programs.
  • NAAC A+ Accreditation and NBA Accredited UG Programs.
  • 19.98 acres of campus with state-of-the-art infrastructure.
  • ISO 9001-2015 Certified Institution.
  • Affiliated to JNTUK and approved by AICTE.
