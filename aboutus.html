Brief Profile

Prasad V Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology, established in 1998, is a pioneering self-financed institution renowned for academic excellence and discipline. The college is sponsored by Siddhartha Academy of General and Technical Education, the premier organization that runs 18 educational institutions in diverse fields of study.

The college offers B.Tech programs in Computer Science Engineering (CSE), CSE - Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (CSM), CSE - Data Science (CSD), Information Technology (IT), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Mechanical Engineering (ME), Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE), and Civil Engineering (CE).

The institution is autonomous, approved by AICTE, permanently affiliated to JNTUK, Kakinada, and accredited by NAAC with A+ grade. All UG programs are accredited by NBA. It is also ISO 9001-2015 certified.

The institution provides ample facilities for research, innovation, and entrepreneurial activities. The placement cell provides career guidance and helps students secure placements, with the highest pay package being 44 lakhs.
